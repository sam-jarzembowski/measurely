Fudgy Brownies

7 ingredients | 9 large or 16 small brownies

10 mins prep | 20 mins bake | 30 mins total

Ingredients

  • 1 stick butter, melted
  • 200g sugar
  • 43g unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 30g all-purpose flour

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350F. Grease an 8x8 square pan or a 9x5 loaf pan or line with parchment or foil; set aside.
  2. In a large microwave-safe mixing bowl, add the butter and microwave for 1 minute or until completely melted. Add the sugar and cocoa powder and whisk vigorously for at least a minute, until the sugar has dissolved.
  3. Add eggs one at a time then vanilla and whisk for at least 1 minute, until well combined and batter is shiny.
  4. Stir in flour until the flour is fully combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  5. Spread in pan and bake for approximately 20 minutes or until the center is slightly set. Be careful not to over-bake!
  6. Cool completely then cut into 9 large squares or 16 small squares.
