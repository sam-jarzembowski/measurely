Sugar Cookies

7 ingredients | 36 servings

15 mins prep | 8 mins bake | 25 mins total

Ingredients

  • 340g all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 2 sticks butter
  • 300g white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven: Preheat the oven to 375F (190C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients: In a small bowl, whisk together 340 grams all-purpose flour and 1 1/4 teaspoons baking soda until combined. Set aside.
  3. Cream butter and sugar: In a large bowl, beat 2 sticks softened butter and 300 grams white sugar together with an electric mixer on medium speed until smooth and fluffy, about 2 minutes.
  4. Add egg and vanilla: Beat in 1 large eggs and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract until fully incorporated.
  5. Add dry ingredients: Gradually blend the flour mixture into the wet ingredients, mixing on low speed until a soft dough forms. Do not overmix.
  6. Shape cookies: Roll rounded teaspoonfuls of dough into balls between your palms. Place them onto ungreased cookie sheets, spacing about 2 inches apart.
  7. Bake: Bake for about 8 minutes, or until the edges are just golden. The centers may look slightly underdone; that's correct.
  8. Cool: Let cookies stand on the cookie sheet for 2 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
